Step 1: Choose an Image
Click the "Choose Image" button. A file dialog will open, allowing you to browse your device. Select the blood smear sample image you want to upload. Once selected, the image file will be prepared for uploading.

Step 2: Preview the Image
After selecting the image, a preview will appear below the "Choose Image" button, allowing you to confirm that the correct image has been uploaded.

Step 3: Click on "Predict"
Once the image is uploaded, click the "Predict" button to start the analysis. The system will begin processing the image.

Step 4: View the Prediction Result
After a short wait, the result will be displayed on the screen, showing whether the sample is infected or not infected with malaria.
