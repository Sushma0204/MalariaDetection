MALARIA DETECTION

This website provides a simple and user-friendly platform where individuals can quickly determine whether they are infected with malaria. By uploading an image of a blood smear, users can receive an automated assessment of their malaria status. You can easily access this feature from the homepage, which is available in the navigation bar or by clicking the button below. This tool is designed to offer fast and reliable malaria detection, empowering users to make informed health decisions.

The Vision Transformer and EfficientNetB0 models stand out as the most effective for automated malaria detection, offering high accuracy and balanced precision-recall tradeoff, making them suitable for resource-limited settings. Additionally, analysis of malaria data from 2010 to 2017 highlights the WHO African region, particularly Uganda, Zambia, and Zimbabwe, as the most affected areas. These findings emphasize the need for robust diagnostic tools and targeted interventions to mitigate malaria's impact effectively.
